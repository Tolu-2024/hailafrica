ABOUT HAIL Africa

Developing African Human Capital Powerhouse to develop African solutions.

Our Story

This initiative envisions an Africa human capital and Powerhouse equipped and developed enough to take on African challenges and solve them without being at the mercy of Non-Africans.

Therefore with research-based, tailored programs, quality mentorship access, relevant partnership. We train Young Africans, allocate them to specialized mentorship and post Young Africans for internship across our partners organization.

And we are not just equipping and developing Young Africans through this solid programs but we also went further to create economic means whereby these trained Africans, and partners can sell themselves to the world on the HAIL Africa Marketplace. Finally our culture is that we must give-back to our communities and raiae other Young Africans which every of our alumni must embark on.
